This Privacy Policy explains how abbieydig.online (the Service) collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use the Service.

1. Who we are

The Service is operated by Abbiey Dig (we, us, our). If you need to contact us about privacy, use the contact details published on abbieydig.online.

2. What the Service does

The Service helps you generate a self-audit report about identifiers you provide (such as an email address, phone number, and online usernames). The Service checks public endpoints and services to produce the report.

3. Information we collect

  • Identifiers you submit
    Such as your name, email address, phone number, and usernames/handles you enter to generate a report.
  • Report data generated by the Service
    Results returned by checks performed using your inputs (for example: DNS results for an email domain, whether a Gravatar exists, profile URL checks, and optional breach results if enabled).
  • Device and usage data
    Basic technical data automatically provided when you access a website, such as IP address, device type, operating system, browser type, language, and timestamps. This data may be used for security, abuse prevention, troubleshooting, and performance.
  • Cookies and similar technologies
    The Service uses cookies that are required for core functionality (for example, maintaining your session). If we introduce optional analytics cookies, we will update this policy and, where required, provide appropriate choices.

4. How we use information

  • Provide the Service
    Generate reports based on the identifiers you provide and display or export them to you.
  • Security and abuse prevention
    Detect fraud, spam, and misuse, and enforce our Terms.
  • Improve and maintain the Service
    Troubleshoot issues, maintain reliability, and improve performance and user experience.

5. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information.

  • Public sites and third-party services you ask us to check
    When you run a scan, the Service may send network requests that include the identifiers you provided (for example, checking whether a username profile URL exists, or querying Gravatar using a one-way hash of an email). If you provide an email address and optional breach checks are enabled, your email address may be sent to Have I Been Pwned to retrieve breach results.
  • Service providers
    Hosting and infrastructure providers, email delivery providers (for email verification), and other vendors used to operate the Service. These providers are only authorized to use information as needed to provide services to us.
  • Legal and safety
    We may disclose information if required to do so by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of the Service, our users, or others.

6. Data retention

Reports and verification codes are intended to be short-lived. We retain generated reports and verification codes only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Service and operate it safely, and may delete or de-identify them earlier. You can also close your browser session to stop using the Service.

7. International transfers

Your information may be processed by service providers in locations outside Australia. Where we disclose personal information overseas, we take reasonable steps to ensure it is handled consistently with this policy and applicable law.

8. Your rights and choices

Depending on your circumstances and applicable law, you may request access to or correction of personal information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us using the details published on abbieydig.online.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
